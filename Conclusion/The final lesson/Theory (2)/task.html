In this course, after a short historical review, we examined the most basic concepts of the JavaScript programming language:


  • The concepts of variables and the simplest operations on them.
  • The main methods of influencing the course of the program are branching and loops.
  • Learned global notions of the object and function.
  • We examined in detail the basic classes that allow you to work with strings, arrays, date and time.
  • Then found out what mathematical functions the Math object supports and tried to work with them.
  • We learned how to handle errors.
  • Have studied what regular expressions are and how to use them.
  • Got the concept of such an entity as a closure.
