Calculation functions:

Math.sqrt(x) - returns the square root of the argument
Math.pow(base, exponent) - raises the number "base" to the power of "exponent"
Math.log(x) - calculates the natural (on the basis of e) logarithm of the number
Math.exp(x) - calculates the exponent - the value of the number e in the power of the argument "x"
Math.random() - returns a random number from 0 (inclusive) to 1
